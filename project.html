Introduction

Cinémur is the main competitor of Allociné, the French largest movie and TV show database. Contrary to its competitor, Cinémur has been designed as a social platform, allowing users to share their movie consumption, watchlist, ratings and reviews. My role at Cinémur was to improve the overall social movie sharing experience, especially on mobile devices. I essentially focused on the iOS application, but also chipped in on the Cinémur website and its Android Application.
